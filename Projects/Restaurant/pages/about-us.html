About Us

Dessert House since 1900

Since our modest beginning in 1900 with a little space in Mumbai’s stylish Worli locale, Dessert House‘s development has been enlivened with the energy to cook and serve solid desserts. In contrast to other eateries, Dessert House was made with the explicit expectation to appear as something else.

This historic place has been the hub of the Indian freedom struggle, and has come a long way since then. Visit our newly renovated eatery soon.
